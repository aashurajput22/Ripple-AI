# 🌊 Ripple AI – Policy Impact Simulator

> Predict • Simulate • Compare • Decide

Ripple AI is an AI-powered Decision Intelligence Platform built for the Google Cloud Agentic AI Day Hackathon 2026.

The platform helps governments, organizations, NGOs, and communities evaluate policies before implementation using Google Gemini AI. Instead of making decisions based on assumptions, Ripple AI predicts impacts, identifies risks, analyzes stakeholders, and provides AI-powered recommendations.

---

## 🚀 Problem Statement

Modern communities generate massive amounts of data, but decision-makers often struggle to transform this information into actionable insights.

Ripple AI bridges this gap by using AI to simulate policy outcomes before implementation.

## 🎯 How It Works

1. User enters a policy or decision scenario.
2. Gemini AI analyzes the scenario.
3. Ripple AI evaluates:
   - Decision Score
   - Risk
   - Stakeholder Impact
   - SDG Alignment
   - Ripple Effects
4. AI generates recommendations and insights.
5. Users can compare policies and export reports.
